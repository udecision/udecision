About

We are passionate about providing decision support feedback in educational settings. Our organization helps young people identify their potential and accompanies them along the path to their professional world.

To reduce uncertainty, we have developed a mobile application that facilitates decision making, providing advice and tracing services of students’s academic itinerary. The application informs about the optimal steps towards a planning aligned with the personal objectives, aptitudes and the future obstacles, sometimes apparently hidden.
